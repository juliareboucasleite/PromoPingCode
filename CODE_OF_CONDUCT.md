Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming
environment for everyone.

Our Standards

Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
Assume good intent and give constructive feedback.
Do not harass, insult, or discriminate.

Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, threats, and abusive language.
Discrimination of any kind.
Publishing private information without permission.

Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to:
juliareboucasleite@gmail.com

Enforcement actions are at the discretion of the project maintainer.
